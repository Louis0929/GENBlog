Fees and Fiat Friction

Both platforms list 0.1% spot trading fees in this data set. The difference is not the headline trading fee; it is the onboarding friction. For Brazil, Pix support matters because a bonus that looks attractive can become less useful if deposits are slow, costly, or require extra identity matching.

Binance has the lower tracked entry deposit in this comparison. Bybit has the higher realistic bonus by $20. The practical question is whether that extra value is worth the deposit, trading-volume and onboarding friction.
